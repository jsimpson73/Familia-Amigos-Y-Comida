Breakfast Tacos

By John Simpson September 18, 2025

Start your day with these delicious breakfast tacos that are as versatile as they are satisfying. Warm corn tortillas filled with fluffy scrambled eggs, crispy potatoes, melted cheese, and your choice of toppings make for a hearty and flavorful breakfast that will keep you energized all morning. These tacos have become a weekend tradition in our home, and they're perfect for feeding a crowd or meal prepping for busy weekday mornings. Customize them with your favorite ingredients for a breakfast that never gets boring!

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes
Servings: 4 (8 tacos)

Ingredients

  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 medium russet potatoes, diced into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper (any color), diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese

For Serving (Optional)

  • Homemade salsa or pico de gallo
  • Sliced avocado or guacamole
  • Sour cream
  • Chopped fresh cilantro
  • Hot sauce
  • Lime wedges
  • Sliced jalapeños

Instructions

  1. In a large non-stick skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of butter over medium heat. Add the diced potatoes and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Add the diced onion and bell pepper to the skillet. Cook for another 5-7 minutes until the potatoes are golden and crispy, and the vegetables are softened.
  3. Add the minced garlic, cumin, chili powder, and paprika. Cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Season the potato mixture with salt and pepper to taste. Transfer to a bowl and cover to keep warm.
  5. In the same skillet, melt the remaining tablespoon of butter over medium-low heat.
  6. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and cook, gently stirring occasionally, until the eggs are softly scrambled but still slightly moist, about 3-4 minutes. Remove from heat.
  8. Warm the corn tortillas according to package directions or directly over a gas flame for a few seconds on each side.
  9. To assemble the tacos, place a spoonful of the potato mixture on each tortilla, followed by a portion of scrambled eggs and a sprinkle of shredded cheese.
  10. Serve immediately with your choice of toppings such as salsa, avocado, sour cream, cilantro, hot sauce, lime wedges, and jalapeños.

Notes

Make-Ahead Option: The potato mixture can be prepared a day in advance and stored in the refrigerator. Reheat in a skillet before assembling the tacos.

Protein Variations: Add cooked and crumbled breakfast sausage, bacon, or chorizo to the potato mixture for extra protein and flavor.

Vegetarian Option: Add black beans or refried beans for a vegetarian protein boost.

Tortilla Tip: If you have time, quickly fry the corn tortillas in a little oil for about 10 seconds per side before assembling. This makes them more pliable and adds flavor.

Meal Prep: Prepare the components separately and store in the refrigerator. In the morning, reheat and assemble for a quick breakfast.
